# Energy Wave
*Catch the momentum wave in the oil and gas sector*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Medium
- **Timeframe**: Short-Medium Term (3-20 days)
- **Target**: Sector Rotator, Macro Trader

Energy Wave filters stocks in the "Energy Minerals" sector by high volume and positive daily change. When oil prices rise or geopolitical tensions increase, the energy sector moves as a whole—this strategy keeps you ahead of that wave.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Sector set as "Energy Minerals," Volume min 500,000, and Change min 0%. Results listed descending by change.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Raise change minimum to 2% to see only strong energy rally stocks.
- Add Market Cap minimum of 10,000M to focus on large energy giants.
- Raise volume minimum to 2M to confirm institutional participation.
- Add max P/E of 15 to filter cheap energy stocks.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
Oil prices rose 4% after an OPEC production cut announcement. XOM, CVX, COP stocks are up 2-5%. Energy Wave lists all these stocks instantly.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ The energy sector is highly dependent on oil prices—track macro developments.
- ⚠️ Geopolitical risks can reverse suddenly.
- ⚠️ The renewable energy trend may pressure fossil fuel companies in the long term.
