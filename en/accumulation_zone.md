# Accumulation Zone
*Price is flat but volume is exploding—track the big players*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Medium
- **Timeframe**: Medium Term (5-20 days)
- **Target**: Technical Analyst, Smart Money Follower

Accumulation Zone identifies stocks where the price barely changed (-0.5% to +0.5%) but volume is over 3 million. This is a sign of "smart money accumulation": institutional investors may be quietly taking positions without moving the price.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Change filtered between -0.5% and +0.5%, Volume min 3,000,000, and Market Cap min 5,000M. Results listed descending by volume.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Change range to -0.2% to +0.2% to see ultra-stable accumulation zones.
- Raise volume minimum to 5M to find mega-volume zones.
- Add Proximity to Peak min of 70% to see accumulation in strong stocks.
- Raise Market Cap min to 20,000M to focus on accumulation in large companies.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
MSFT rose only 0.1% today but saw 8M volume (2x normal). Price is flat, but someone is quietly buying large amounts. Accumulation Zone catches this "invisible" signal.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ High volume + low price action could also be distribution—direction is uncertain.
- ⚠️ Action after an accumulation signal can take days or weeks—patience required.
- ⚠️ Volume can also bloat for technical reasons like options expiration.
