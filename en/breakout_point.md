# Breakout Point
*Detect the classic breakout formation live*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: High
- **Timeframe**: Short Term (1-5 days)
- **Target**: Technical Analyst, Breakout Trader

Breakout Point combines three critical filters: 90%+ proximity to peak, 1M+ volume, and positive daily change. This combination is the signal flare for a classic "breakout" in technical analysis.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Proximity to Peak min 90%, Volume min 1,000,000, and Change min +0.1%. Results listed descending by proximity.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Raise proximity to 95% to see the most mature breakout candidates.
- Raise volume minimum to 2M to strengthen institutional confirmation.
- Raise change minimum to 1% to see who is breaking out with strong momentum.
- Add Market Cap minimum of 5,000M to focus on breakouts in larger companies.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
AMZN is 93% near its peak, rose 1.8% today with 8M volume. All three criteria met—classic breakout formation. Entry point.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Not every breakout signal turns into a real breakout—fakeouts are common.
- ⚠️ Check general market direction (SPY, QQQ).
- ⚠️ Be careful if an earnings date is near—volatility increases.
