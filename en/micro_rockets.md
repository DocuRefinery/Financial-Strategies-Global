# Micro Rockets
*Hunt for small stocks with explosive growth potential*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Very High
- **Timeframe**: Short Term (1-3 days)
- **Target**: Speculator, Risk-Loving Trader

Micro Rockets finds small market-cap stocks showing strong gains and high volume today. These stocks offer speculative opportunities—potential for large returns with small capital, but risks are equally high.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Market Cap maximum 2,000M, Change minimum +3%, and Volume minimum 500,000. Results are listed descending by change percentage.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Lower Market Cap maximum to 500M to see nano/micro-cap rockets.
- Raise minimum change to 7% to track only the most aggressive rallies.
- Add a price filter to focus on stocks between $5-$50.
- Leave P/E blank to include growth companies that aren't profitable yet.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
A biotech company ($800M cap) rose 12% with 3M volume after FDA approval. Micro Rockets brings this stock to the top of the list instantly.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ This is the highest-risk strategy—loss potential is very high.
- ⚠️ Be wary of pump & dump schemes.
- ⚠️ Limit position size to 2-5% of your portfolio.
- ⚠️ Use a stop-loss in the 5-8% range.
