# Finance Play
*Hunt for value opportunities in banking and finance giants*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Low-Medium
- **Timeframe**: Medium-Long Term (15-90 days)
- **Target**: Value Investor, Sector Specialist

Finance Play finds companies in the "Finance" sector with a low P/E ratio (under 15) and a market cap over $10 billion. Banks, insurers, and finance giants usually trade at low P/E—this strategy filters for the sector's top value opportunities.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Sector as "Finance," P/E 0.1-15, and Market Cap min 10,000M. Results listed ascending by P/E.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Raise Market Cap min to 50,000M to see only mega banks.
- Lower max P/E to 10 to see ultra-cheap finance giants.
- Add change minimum of +1 to focus on rising finance stocks.
- Add Proximity to Peak min of 70% to see finance giants trading strongly.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
The banking sector revives after a FED rate hike. BAC (Bank of America, $300B, P/E 11) stands out with net interest income growth. Finance Play detects this opportunity.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ The finance sector is very sensitive to interest rates—follow FED decisions closely.
- ⚠️ Banking crises are contagious—one bank's problem can drag down the sector.
- ⚠️ Regulatory changes can affect profitability suddenly.
