# Silent Giants
*Mega-cap fortresses for investors seeking a safe harbor*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Low
- **Timeframe**: Long Term (3-12 months)
- **Target**: Conservative Investor, Retirement Portfolio

Silent Giants finds stocks with a market cap over $100 billion and daily price changes between -2% and +2%. These are stable mega-cap companies least affected by market fluctuations.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Market Cap minimum 100,000M and Change filtered between -2% and +2%. Results listed descending by market cap.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Raise Market Cap minimum to 500,000M to see only the world's top 20 companies.
- Set Change range to -1% to +1% to filter ultra-calm giants.
- Add a max P/E of 25 to eliminate overvalued tech giants.
- Set sector filter to "Consumer Non-Durables" to see defensive giants.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
The market is down 3%, but JNJ (Johnson & Johnson, $400B) only fell 0.3%. Silent Giants shows this stable stock—a safe harbor.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Low volatility = low short-term return potential.
- ⚠️ Even mega-caps can be affected by sector crises.
- ⚠️ Think of this as a long-term strategy—not suitable for day trading.
