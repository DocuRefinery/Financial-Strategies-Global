# Cash Machine
*Find giants that generate regular cash with steady profitability*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Low
- **Timeframe**: Long Term (6-24 months)
- **Target**: Income Investor, Retirement Planner

Cash Machine finds companies with a P/E of 5-15 (strong profitability), market cap over $30 billion, stable price action (-1% to +1%), and 50-80% proximity to peak. This profile points to stable giants producing regular cash flow and not overvalued.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
P/E 5-15, Market Cap min 30,000M, Change -1% to +1%, and Proximity to Peak 50-80%. Results listed ascending by P/E.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Lower max P/E to 10 to see the most profitable giants.
- Raise Market Cap min to 100,000M to focus on mega-cap cash machines.
- Change range to -0.5% to +0.5% to filter ultra-stable stocks.
- Set sector to "Utilities" to see infrastructure cash machines.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
VZ (Verizon, $170B, P/E 9) stable price, strong cash flow, regular dividends. 65% near peak—neither overpriced nor crashed. Cash Machine lists such "boring but profitable" giants.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Low P/E + large market cap can mean stagnant growth.
- ⚠️ Stable price action could also be a lack of interest in the stock.
- ⚠️ Sectoral disruption risk (tech changes, etc.) is the biggest long-term threat.
