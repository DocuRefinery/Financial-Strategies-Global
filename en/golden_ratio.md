# Golden Ratio
*Find balanced "Goldilocks" stocks across all metrics*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Low-Medium
- **Timeframe**: Medium Term (10-45 days)
- **Target**: Balanced Investor, Portfolio Builder

Golden Ratio finds stocks that aren't extreme in any metric: reasonable P/E (10-20), mid market cap ($5-50B), moderate rise (0.5-5%), reasonable distance to peak (60-85%), and sufficient volume. This Goldilocks strategy is the point of balance, not extreme risk but with potential.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
P/E 10-20, Market Cap 5,000-50,000M, Change 0.5-5%, Proximity to Peak 60-85%, and Volume min 500,000. Results listed descending by change.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- P/E range 12-18 for a narrower valuation band.
- Proximity range 70-80% to focus on a more specific area.
- Market Cap range 10,000-30,000M to narrow the "sweet spot" range.
- Lower change limit to 3% to eliminate extreme volatility.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
ETR (Entergy, $25B, P/E 16) rose 2% today, 73% near peak, with 700K volume. All metrics in balance—neither ultra-cheap nor ultra-expensive, neither risky nor boring. The ideal profile sought by Golden Ratio.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Balanced metrics mean lower risk but require time for significant returns.
- ⚠️ This strategy might be too slow for those seeking aggressive returns.
- ⚠️ If the general market falls, balanced stocks are also affected—correlation is high.
