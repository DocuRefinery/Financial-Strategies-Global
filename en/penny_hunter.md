# Penny Hunter
*Discover explosive opportunities in ultra-low price stocks*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Very High
- **Timeframe**: Short Term (1-3 days)
- **Target**: Speculator, High Risk Trader

Penny Hunter identifies stocks priced under $5, up more than 5%, with volume over 1 million. Penny stocks are the riskiest but offer the highest potential return (or loss). This strategy is only for experienced traders with high risk tolerance.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Price max $5, Change min +5%, and Volume min 1,000,000. Results listed descending by change.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Lower price limit to $3 to see ultra-penny stocks.
- Raise change minimum to 10% to see only the most explosive moves.
- Raise volume minimum to 3M to filter for penny stocks with more reliable liquidity.
- Set a price floor of $1 to eliminate sub-penny traps.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
A biotech penny stock ($2.50) announced FDA Phase 3 results and rose 25% with 5M volume. Penny Hunter catches such explosive moves instantly.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Penny stocks are the most dangerous investment category—you can lose all capital.
- ⚠️ Pump & dump, manipulation, and fake news are very common.
- ⚠️ Spreads can be very wide—the bid-ask difference can eat your profit.
- ⚠️ Allocate maximum 1-2% of your total portfolio.
