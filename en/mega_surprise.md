# Mega Surprise
*Catch unusual big moves in giant companies*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Medium
- **Timeframe**: Short Term (1-5 days)
- **Target**: News-Based Trader, Event Follower

Mega Surprise identifies daily price moves over 3% in giant companies with market caps over $50 billion. Mega-caps normally move 0.5-1%—a 3%+ move means earnings, mergers, regulation, or a sector earthquake.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Market Cap min 50,000M and Change min +3%. Results listed descending by change.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Raise change minimum to 5% to see only "shock" level mega moves.
- Raise Market Cap min to 200,000M to see surprises only in the world's largest companies.
- Add a volume filter to check if the move is confirmed by volume.
- Add a sector filter to focus on mega surprises in a specific sector.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
GOOGL ($1.8T) rose 5% today—reported much better earnings than expected. Mega Surprise detects this unusual move instantly. When giants move, the sector follows.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Mega-cap surprises are often earnings-related—momentum might be short-lived.
- ⚠️ Entering after a big rise carries "late entry" risk.
- ⚠️ The surprise can also be downward—don't think one-sidedly.
