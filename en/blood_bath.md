# Blood Bath
*Catch contrarian opportunities when everyone is selling*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Very High
- **Timeframe**: Medium Term (5-30 days)
- **Target**: Contrarian Trader, Value Hunter

Blood Bath lists stocks that fall more than 5% in a day. It's the core of a contrarian strategy: searching for opportunity where the crowd is panic selling. A digital application of "Buy when there is blood in the streets."

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Change (%) maximum -5% and Volume minimum 500,000. Results are listed in ascending order (most dropped at the top).

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Lower the change maximum to -10% to see move-level crashes.
- Add Market Cap minimum of 10,000M to focus on drops in large companies.
- Add a P/E filter to separate profitable but falling companies.
- Add Proximity to Peak minimum of 60% to see stocks that were strong but fell today.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
JPM (JP Morgan) fell 7% on unexpected regulation news. The market is selling in panic, but basics are solid. Blood Bath shows this opportunity instantly.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Trying to catch a falling knife is dangerous—the reason for the drop is critical.
- ⚠️ Don't enter without reading the news behind the drop.
- ⚠️ Apply a Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA) strategy—don't go all-in at once.
- ⚠️ Always consider the possibility of a "dead cat bounce."
