# Dividend Fortress
*Stocks with high dividend potential for regular income seekers*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Low
- **Timeframe**: Long Term (6-24 months)
- **Target**: Dividend Investor, Passive Income Seeker

Dividend Fortress finds companies with a market cap over $50 billion and a P/E ratio under 18. Companies in this profile are usually mature, profitable giants that pay regular dividends.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Market Cap minimum 50,000M and P/E ratio between 0.1-18. Results listed descending by market cap.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Lower P/E limit to 12 to see cheaper dividend candidates.
- Raise Market Cap minimum to 100,000M to see only mega-cap dividend giants.
- Set sector to "Utilities" or "Finance" to scan classic dividend sectors.
- Add Change filter -1% / +1% to see giants with calm price action.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
PG (Procter & Gamble, $350B, P/E 13) has raised dividends for 65 consecutive years. Dividend Fortress helps you filter such "Dividend Aristocrat" companies.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ This screener doesn't show dividend yield—research dividend history separately.
- ⚠️ Low P/E + large market cap doesn't always mean high dividends.
- ⚠️ Check payout ratio to distinguish companies at risk of a dividend cut.
