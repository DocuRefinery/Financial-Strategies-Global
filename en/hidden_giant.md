# Hidden Giant
*Discover mid-cap growth candidates everyone is missing*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Medium
- **Timeframe**: Medium Term (5-30 days)
- **Target**: Growth Hunter, Active Investor

The Hidden Giant strategy finds stocks with a market cap between $2-20 billion, valued at a low P/E ratio, and staying strong at over 75% of their 52-week peak. These companies aren't as visible as mega-caps but have strong fundamentals—growth machines off the market radar.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Market Cap between 2,000M-20,000M, P/E 0.1-15, Proximity to Peak min 75%, and Change min +0.1%. Results listed ascending by P/E.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Set Market Cap range to 5,000-15,000M for a more selective mid-cap scan.
- Lower P/E limit to 10 to see ultra-cheap hidden giants.
- Raise proximity minimum to 85% to find candidates closest to breakout.
- Change sector filter to search for hidden giants in specific sectors.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
DECK (Deckers Outdoor, $15B) has a P/E of 12 and is 82% near its peak. Not talked about as much as mega-caps, but breaking sales records with the UGG brand. Hidden Giant surfaces such opportunities.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Mid-cap stocks are more volatile than mega-caps.
- ⚠️ Low P/E + mid-cap could signal slowing growth—check revenue trends.
- ⚠️ Liquidity might be lower than mega-caps—watch the spread on large positions.
