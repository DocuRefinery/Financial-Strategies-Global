# Peak Hunter
*Discover stocks preparing to break their 52-week high*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: High
- **Timeframe**: Short-Medium Term (1-15 days)
- **Target**: Breakout Trader, Momentum Trader

Peak Hunter finds stocks trading above 95% of their 52-week high. These stocks are candidates for a "breakout." In technical analysis, breaking a 52-week high is considered a strong bullish signal.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Filters the "Proximity to Peak" column in the Screener for a minimum of 95%. Results are listed in descending order of proximity.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Raise the proximity threshold to 98% to see stocks only at the breakout edge.
- Add a minimum of 500,000 volume to eliminate low liquidity noise.
- Set Market Cap to a minimum of 1,000M to filter out small company noise.
- Add a minimum of +0.5 to Change (%) to see only candidates rising today.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
AAPL is trading at 97% of its 52-week peak with above-average volume. This is a signal to test the peak. Historically, 60% of stocks at 95%+ proximity set a new peak within 10 days.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Stocks near peaks can be "bull traps."
- ⚠️ Individual breakouts often fail during a general market decline.
- ⚠️ Always set a stop-loss—peak rejection can lead to sharp drops.
