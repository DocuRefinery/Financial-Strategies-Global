# Tech Squeeze
*Catch squeeze and rally signals in the tech sector*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Medium-High
- **Timeframe**: Short-Medium Term (3-15 days)
- **Target**: Sector Rotator, Tech Follower

Tech Squeeze filters stocks in the "Technology Services" sector by high volume and proximity to their peak. It is designed to detect periods when the sector as a whole is squeezing and preparing for a rally.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Sector filtered as "Technology Services," Volume minimum 1,000,000, Change minimum 0%, and Proximity to Peak minimum 85%. Results listed descending by volume.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Change sector to "Electronic Technology" to scan hardware and semiconductor companies.
- Raise proximity to 95% to see breakout candidates from sector leaders.
- Raise volume minimum to 5M to track only mega-volume tech stocks.
- Add max P/E of 40 to eliminate overvalued growth stocks.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
The tech sector is squeezed ahead of a FED rate decision. MSFT, GOOGL, META are all 90%+ near peaks with high volume. This is a classic squeeze before a sector rally.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Sector squeezes are heavily influenced by macro factors.
- ⚠️ FED decisions and inflation data affect the tech sector disproportionately.
- ⚠️ Concentrating too much in one sector hurts portfolio diversification.
