# Health Shield
*Discover crisis-resistant health sector giants*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: Low
- **Timeframe**: Long Term (3-12 months)
- **Target**: Defensive Investor, Portfolio Diversifier

Health Shield filters companies in the "Health Services" sector with a market cap over $5 billion. The health sector is defensive, seeing demand even in economic crises—people spend on health even in a recession.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Sector filtered as "Health Services," Market Cap min 5,000M, and Volume min 500,000. Results listed descending by market cap.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Set sector to "Health Technology" to scan biotech and medical device companies.
- Raise Market Cap min to 20,000M to focus only on health giants.
- Add max P/E of 25 to see health stocks with reasonable valuation.
- Add change minimum of +0.5 to focus on rising health stocks.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
Economic recession fears rise. UNH (UnitedHealth, $450B) remains limited to -0.5% while the market falls. Health Shield lists such defensive giants.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ The health sector is prone to regulatory risk—drug price controls are on the political agenda.
- ⚠️ The biotech sub-sector is very volatile—"Health Services" is more defensive.
- ⚠️ Patent expirations can lead to revenue loss.
