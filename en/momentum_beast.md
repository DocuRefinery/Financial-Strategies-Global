# Momentum Beasts
*Trade the trend—catch strong rallies*

## 1. What is this Strategy?
- **Risk Level**: High
- **Timeframe**: Short Term (1-5 days)
- **Target**: Momentum Trader, Swing Trader

Momentum Beasts finds stocks that rose more than 5% today with over 1 million volume. Momentum strategies rely on the assumption that a strong trend will continue.

## 2. Auto-Filter Parameters
When you click this card, the following filters are automatically applied:

| Column | Applied Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| Live Volume | > 1.000.000 |
| Market Cap | > 10B |

## 3. How It Works?
Change minimum +5% and Volume minimum 1,000,000. Results listed descending by change.

## 4. Manual Tuning Guide
You can manually change the filters to fit your style:
- Raise change minimum to 8% to see only rally-level gains.
- Raise volume minimum to 3M to confirm institutional participation.
- Add Proximity to Peak minimum of 80% to find momentum stocks already in a trend.
- Add Market Cap minimum of 5,000M to focus on momentum in larger companies.

## 5. Real-World Scenario
TSLA rose 8% today with 15M volume. New factory announcement. Momentum Beasts catches this signal—the trend may continue.

## 6. Risks & Warnings
- ⚠️ Momentum strategies are prone to "late entry" risk.
- ⚠️ Profit taking after an extreme rise can be sharp.
- ⚠️ Use a trailing stop-loss to protect your profits.
- ⚠️ Don't confuse general market momentum with an individual stock.
