A deterministic, multi-language market scanner architecture indexing 5,000+ US equities in real-time, executing 24 mathematically defined investment strategies across 16 languages.

This repository serves as the public documentation protocol and strategic blueprint for [DocuRefinery.com]. 

We engineered DocuRefinery to democratize the heavy-lifting quantitative frameworks historically reserved for institutional trading desks. Our approach strips away speculative forecasting and replaces it with cold, hard algorithmic screening—allowing any investor to identify volume anomalies, value discrepancies, and momentum shifts instantly.

## Core Principles

- **Zero Arbitrary Data:** Strategies are strictly bound to mathematical rulesets based on Price/Earnings variations, real-time volume influx, market capitalization, and 52-week proximity.
- **Completely Free Infrastructure:** No paywalls, no subscriptions, no credit cards. The entire screening engine is open to the public.
- **Not a SaaS:** We reject the recurring billing models of legacy software providers.
- **No Registration Ecosystem:** No accounts, no email harvesting, no tracking. Privacy is absolute; you open the terminal and begin your analysis.

## Algorithmic Strategy Engine

One of the defining barriers in modern finance is the complexity of data parsing. Instead of forcing users to build algorithmic filters from scratch, this framework pre-packages 24 distinct, institutional-grade logic sets. 

Here is how the engine deploys constraints:

- **Volume Explosion:** Scans strictly for equities bypassing the 2-million volume threshold combined with dynamic descending sorts, instantly surfacing institutional accumulation points.
- **Cash Machine:** Narrowly evaluates mega-cap entities ($30B+) operating within a rigid 5-15 P/E ratio band and tight volatility control (-1% to +1%), isolating highly robust dividend candidates.
- **Bull Trap Protocol:** Identifies false breakout scenarios by filtering assets dangerously close to their 52-week highs (95%+) that are simultaneously recording sudden negative intraday shifts.

## Quick Start Protocol

The friction from raw market data to actionable conviction has been reduced to three immediate actions:

1. **Visit [DocuRefinery.com]**.
2. **Deploy a Strategy** via a single click on the dashboard (e.g., *Peak Hunter*, *Blood Bath*, *Dividend Fortress*). The platform forces internal screening parameters instantly.
3. **Execute AI Diligence:** Click on any successfully filtered asset to launch a multi-model (Claude, Gemini, Qwen) deterministic evaluation of intrinsic value.
